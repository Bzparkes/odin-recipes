Lasagna

A delicious lasagna

Required ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups of shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 can of artichokes
  • 8 ounces of shredded mozzarella
  • 3 qtr cup parmesan
  • Half cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 packages of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of dired basil
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 8 ounces of mozzarella

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients (except lasange noodles, mozzarella & parmesan) in a large bowl and thoroughly mix.
  2. Once ingredients are mixed, cover bottom of baking dish with pasta sheets. Spoon mixture on top and layer sheets. Repeat 2 more times, then cover with cheese.
  3. Put in pre-heated oven of 180 degrees and cook for 40 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
